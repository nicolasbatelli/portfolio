This is the first post on my portfolio blog. I'll use this space to share thoughts on software development, 3D design, and the projects I'm working on.

## What to expect

- **Project write-ups** — deep dives into things I've built
- **3D printing notes** — design tips, print settings, and lessons learned
- **Technical tutorials** — things I wish I'd known earlier

## Built with

This site runs on **Next.js**, **Tailwind CSS**, and **shadcn/ui**, deployed on Vercel. The 3D viewer uses Three.js via React Three Fiber.

Feel free to explore the rest of the site, and check back for new posts!
